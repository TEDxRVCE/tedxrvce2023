CEREBRAL REFLECTION

An aim to provide a platform for ideas that would not have seen the light of day otherwise. Ranging from microplastics to the education system, from inspiration to international relations, the talks covered a wide range of topics to reflect on.

SPEAKERS

Dr. Arvind Kasaragod

Presently the Director Medical Services for the Cloudnine group of hospitals, he has been in been in practice from the past 32 years. After completing his MBBS from the University of Madras, he went abroad to broaden his horizons. He was a part of American Board of Pediatrics and then became a member of the Royal College of Physicians in Pediatrics in the UK. He has published numerous research articles in reputed medical journals, penned a chapter for a book and given scientific talks in prestigious conferences in India, USA, and Canada. He has also been instrumental in saving many lives by donating medical equipment to government and underfunded missionary hospitals.In this TEDx talk Dr.Arvind talks about what goes on inside a doctor's brain in making those life-changing decisions for their patients, the moral dilemma that arises and the qualities that attribute to making a credible doctor.

Maneesha Ramakrishnan

She has braved through the tragic Carlton Towers fire accident in 2010. Her talk revolved around her brave journey and resilience through her recovery from the incident.

Alok Prasanna

A senior resident Fellow and Team Lead, Vidhi Karnataka, a graduate of NALSAR University and University of Oxford, he spoke on our Uniform Civil Code and the de-merits and merits of its existence.

Advait Kumar

He is the Co-Founder of SWAJAL, a start-up presently overseeing more than 500 water purification installations from airports to villages. He educated us on the consumption of micro plastics and its damaging effects on us and the environment.

Arjun Singar

The co-founder of a CBSE school: CREAA Academy for Learning, a member of PES, Mandya& an assistant Professor from RVCE, he spoke about his newest feature a contemporary learning model for higher education named FRIYAY.

Piyush Goswami

In this TEDx Talk, Mr. Piyush Goswami speaks about what makes one's creative expression truly their own. He illustrates his points by taking pages out of his decade long life on the road that began with him taking up documentary photography and led him to establish a social-work-through-art organization called "Rest Of My Family"(ROMF). As a part of the organization, Mr. Goswami and his team live with various remote and ignored rural, tribal communities, documents their situation on the ground and after careful personal experience and study initiate relevant social development programs to empower these communities. By taking us through his own personal journey of discovering his true voice, he talks about how every artist, irrespective of their subjective field, can go about discovering their true selves. He was acknowledged as one of the Heroes of Philanthropy in 2018 by Forbes India.

Dr.Thushara James

She is a Government Pleader Of the State of Kerala to the High Court of Kerala. Her law firm, TJ Legal specialises in tax, shipping, contracts and arbitration. Her talk revolves around the jurisdiction and legality of the sea.

Dr. Vaishali Raghuvanshi

An Assistant Professor of international Relations at Christ University, Bangalore and a PhD holder from the South Asian University in International relations, she spoke about the IndoPak Equation: Mental Borders and the influence of Cinema.

Gurucharan V

"Jack of all trades, master of none is oftentimes better than a master of one.” Upholding the tradition of TEDxRVCE presenting to you all the student speaker of the year, Gurucharan V,a CA final year student, cricket enthusiast, melophile and flutist who never wants to stop honing his skills. A strong believer in connections above anything else, a keen seeker of nuisances in things and a bigger enthusiast of sharing his experiences through his speech, poetry, and writing.What do you think is the tiniest thing you've consciously done? ever thought of how important the angle of your staple pin can be? or anything else about "the art of document presentation"? In this TEDx talk Mr. Gurucharan talks about the various important rules of document presentation, that may be overlooked by people in daily life but play a vital role in your professional impression.
