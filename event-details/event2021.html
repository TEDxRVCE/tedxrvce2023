SPEAKERS

Rhea Mazumder Singhal

Rhea Mazumder Singhal is the founder and CEO of Ecoware, India’s first and largest sustainable packaging company. She is also the recipient of India’s highest civilian honour for women, the Nari Shakti Puraskar. At TEDxRVCE 2021, she will be speaking about her motivation to embark upon a journey of creating a natural and eco-friendly alternative to commoditized single-use plastic at Ecoware and sustainability

Brig. Dr. Arvind Lal

Dr. Arvind Lal is a world-renowned pathologist, medical administrator and the chairman and managing director of Dr. Lal PathLabs, which is a medical diagnostic center in Delhi. He is a medical graduate, a medical academic and holds the rank of a Brigadier in the Indian Armed Forces, an honorary rank conferred by the Army. He is also the recipient of the Padma Shri, India’s fourth highest civilian honor, for his contribution to Medicine. He will be speaking about the pathology side of healthcare during the COVID-19 pandemic at TEDxRVCE 2021.

Raj Abhisar Agarwal

Raj Abhisar is a content creator on Instagram. He is experienced in Financial Analysis, Economics and Research. Currently pursuing Masters of Arts in Economics at Boston University, he is a student speaker for our event, TEDxRVCE 2021, where he will be speaking about the financial responsibility of students.

Lucie Fink

Lucie Beatrice Fink is a content creator on YouTube and a lifestyle host. She began her journey of content creation while still a college student at John Hopkins University. Her content encompasses a wide array of subjects. At the TEDxRVCE 2021, she will be speaking about tackling negativity

Dr. Debashree Dattaray

Dr. Debashree Dattaray is an associate professor of comparative literature at Jadavpur University. She has also been a visiting professor at numerous universities all over the world, including Humboldt University, Berlin and University of California, Berkeley. Numerous book chapters and journal articles have also been authored and published by her. Her primary area of studies is the indigenous communities of North-Eastern India, on which she has and continues to conduct extensive research. She will also be speaking on the same at TEDxRVCE2021.
