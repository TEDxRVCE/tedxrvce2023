RESONATING CHORDS

Like the resonant notes of a perfectly tuned instrument, we beleive that when we come together, our collective voices can create a symphony of inspiration and change. Join us on a journey of harmonious ideas, profound connections, and inspiring voices. Let's strike the perfect chord together!

SPEAKERS

Rishi Raghavan

Founding President of Bengaluru Nava Yuva and Governing Council Member of Bengaluru Nava Nirmana Party

Rishvanjas Raghavan, affectionately known as Rishi! At the youthful age of 23, Rishi breaks the mold as a civic politician hailing from Bengaluru, without any family background in politics. His educational journey includes a Masters in Public Policy from National Law School Bengaluru, equipping him with a deep understanding of governance. As the Founding President of Bengaluru Nava Yuva, a youth-centric political society, Rishi's focus is to actively engage the city's youngsters in local governance.

Andaleeb Wajid

Novelist

Andaleeb Wajid is a hybrid author, having published 40 novels in the past 14 years. Andaleeb enjoys writing in a number of different genres such as young adult, romance, and horror. Andaleeb's romance trilogy Jasmine Villa Series was published by Westland in February 2023. She has an upcoming YA horror novel with Harper Collins later in 2023.

Nishit Sharma

Travel Filmmaker

Nishit is a travel filmmaker, self-taught cinematographer, Youtuber and an IITian who is living his passion to travel and filmmaking while shaking digital marketing at a London firm. Along with his job, he is making Travel videos on his YouTube channel which goes with the name Hopping Bug- with 300K subscribers. His work has been featured in BBC, few Bollywood movies such as Brahmastra. He is also working and ambassador for various travel and filmmaking brands like Apple, Sony, Incredible India, Kazakhstan tourism, Thailand Tourism, Assam Tourism, Ritz Carlton, Revv, Tripoto, Tripver, GoIbibo to name a few. Nishit is also known as the "drone man of India" for his varied skills in operating various type of drones and for being one of the few certified FPV pilots in India.

Krishna Shetty

Artist, Printmaker, Columnist and an Art critic. An honorary member of the Russian Academy of Arts, Recipient of the Vincent Van Gogh award.

Krishna Shetty is a highly decorated artist, art critic, organizer, Administrator and writer. He has been a former Administrator and Chairman, Lalit Kala Akademi, New Delhi & Chairman, Karnataka Lalithakala Academy. With many accolades, awards and exhibitions to his credit, is a many of many many talents. We can't wait to hear what all he has to share with us, and look forward to gaining new perspectives to life and art with his talk.

Dr Padmakshi Lokesh

Clinical Psychologist, Behaviour and Stress management specialist and Keynote Speaker on women empowerment and mental health.

With expertise in clinical psychology, education, stress management, and personality development, Dr. Lokesh brings over 15 years of experience. Her motto: "Nothing Ventured, Nothing Gained." She holds key positions in various organizations and has received numerous awards for her outstanding contributions.

Sujay Shanbhag

Bharatnatyam Dancer

Vidwan Sujay Shanbhag is a talented Bharatanatyam artist who has also studied Kuchipudi and is currently pursuing his learning under Guru Padma Subramanyam. Sujay's training in Carnatic vocal music, Mridangam, and Kalaripayattu has further honed his abilities. He has received critical acclaim for his Rangapravesham performances in Yakshagana and Bharatanatyam. Sujay has also showcased his acting skills in various dramas and received the Best Actor Award for his role in the Konkani movie 'Anthu.' As the Managing Trustee of Kala Sujaya, he is dedicated to promoting Bharatiya Classical Art and nurturing young talent. The trust conducts Bharatanatyam classes and hosts regular dance festivals, showcasing Sujay's commitment to the progress of the art form.
